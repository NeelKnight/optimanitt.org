Instructions
Register

How to Proceed

1.  Register yourself from the register tab.
2.  Confirm your registration by entering the confirmation code sent to your registered e-mail.
3.  Make sure you have read all given instructions carefully before you proceed for event selection.
4.  Choose the event you want to participate in. Remember that participation in some events are mutually exclusive.
5.  Be sure to check out our FAQs section for queries.



Rules and regulations

1.  An individual cannot participate in more than one event amongst Rubber Duck, Adbiz, Webmistri, IQ Maze, and Battle of Minds.
2.  An individual cannot be a member of more than one team.
3.  If an individual is shortlisted in the events - Info Fiesta or Pixethon, s/he cannot continue in any other event.
4.  Spot registration is not allowed.
5.  No registration fee is required for any event.
6.  ID card is mandatory, and without it, participation will not be allowed in any event.
7.  Decision of the judges will be final and binding. No disputes will be entertained.
8.  Any act against the rules and regulations of the respective events will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
9.  Use of any electronic device while participating in an event is not allowed.
10. All team members must be from the same institution.



FAQs

Q. I am a PG student. Can I participate?
A. Yes, all our events are open for students from both undergraduate as well as postgraduate courses.

Q. Is there any reward for event winners?
A. Yes! We have cash prizes for winners from every event.

Q. What is the registration fee for the events enlisted?
A. All our events are free for participation. No registration fee is required. Come show us your ardour.

Q. Is there any token of appreciation for participants?
A. Yes, we will provide participation certificates to everyone.

Q. Is there anything else besides the events?
A. Yes, we have free workshops for you to attend on site. There will also be a guest lecture that you won't want to miss.

Q. Is there any provision made for meals?
A. Yes, we have made arrangements for lunch.

Q. Am I required to register for the events - Info Fiesta, Pixethon, or, Adbiz?
A. Yes, one must register for any event that s/he wants to participate in. It will enable us to make arrangements for your lunch and prepare your participation certificates.
